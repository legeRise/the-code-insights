Good software writing should make a difficult idea feel approachable. This site is where I collect the explanations, experiments, and lessons I want to remember.

## Markdown is enough

Every article here begins as a plain Markdown file. There is no editor to fight and no database to maintain—just words, examples, and the occasional diagram.

```js
const insight = "Small ideas, explained carefully.";
console.log(insight);
```

Replace this sample with your first article when you are ready.
